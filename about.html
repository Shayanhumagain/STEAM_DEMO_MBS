About Us


Millsberry School provides high school education to students in an effort to transform their lives for the better.
Millsberry School strives to offer affordable and high-quality education to individuals. As part of the institution's mission to provide the highest quality education,
it strives to cultivate a respectful and transparent academic environment that promotes creativity and collaboration.
The development and sharing of expertise is critical to improving our capacity for delivering our aims,
as well as fostering professional relationships and embracing the idea of inclusion.

Over the years, the School has maintained a reputation for academic excellence and professionalism aligned with the International Organization for Standardization (ISO). Millsberry has also been evaluated for its quality since 2012. Since 2014, Millsberry has been promoting various research and innovative initiatives to integrate international curriculum with Nepal government curriculum (CDC).
